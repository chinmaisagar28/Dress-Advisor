Getting Started

Welcome to Ai Dress Advisor! Follow these steps to use our application:

  1. Choose the gender for which you want to find outfit recommendations.
  2. Select the occasion for which you need outfit suggestions.
  3. Upload an image of yourself or the person you're seeking advice for.
  4. Click on the "Upload" button to submit the image for analysis.
  5. View the color analysis results and advised dresses.
